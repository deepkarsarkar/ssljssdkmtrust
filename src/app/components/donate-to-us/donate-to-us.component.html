SOME IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  • Instruction for Indian Donors
    • The minimum donation for online donation is ₹. 100/-
    • PAN or Aadhaar Number is required for donations below ₹. 10,000/-
      • PAN is mandatory for donations above ₹. 10,000/-
      • Enter PAN in UPPERCASE
    • You will receive a donation acknowledgement receipt, after a successful transaction, from donations.ssljssdkmtt@gmail.com
    • Please make sure to enter the correct details for Name, Address, Email, PAN or Aadhaar Number.
      • If the email is mistyped, you may not receive the donation acknowledgement receipt email.
    • Please not enter multiple names in the First Name and Last Name fields. Make use of the Message from Donors field to enter additional information, if any.
    • If the transaction is successful and you don't see the email in your inbox you may please check your spam folder.
    • For any help regarding online donations, please contact us at donations.ssljssdkmtt@gmail.com and also enter the title of the email as Query Regarding Online Donations
  • Instruction for International Donors
    • The minimum donation for online donation is $ 5
    • Passport Number / Tax Identification Number is mandatory

It will create a problem for us from the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India. Citizenship is the key point for ascertaining a contribution whether it is Foreign Contribution or an Indian Contribution.
