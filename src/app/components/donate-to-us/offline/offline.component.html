some important information

  • Donations to Sri Sri Lakshmi Janardana & Sri Sri Dakshina Kali Mata Temple Trust may be sent through Cheques, Demand Drafts or Money Orders.
  • Account payee cheques and Demand Drafts should be drawn in favour of Sri Sri Lakshmi Janardana & Sri Sri Dakshina Kali Mata Temple Trust.
  • Please DO NOT forget to fill up the donations form. Please note that the form MUST be submitted before the cheque or demand draft is realised.
    • It is advisable to fillup the form before sending the cheque or demand draft or money order.
  • Donations to Sri Sri Lakshmi Janardana & Sri Sri Dakshina Kali Mata Temple Trust is used for the following purposes:
    • Daily Worship (Nitya Seva)
    • Various Periodic Special Pujas on Purnima, Amavasya, Ekadashi & other days.
    • Kali Puja
    • Maintainance of the Temples.
