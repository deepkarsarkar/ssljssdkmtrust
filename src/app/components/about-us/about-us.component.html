Welcome to the official website of Sri Sri Lakshmi Janardana & Sri Sri Dakshina Kali Mata temple Trust. It is a non-decript pair of temples that are maintained by the family of the Kar Sarkars. It is located in the village of Gangadaspur, near the town of Kshirpai, under the Chandrakona Police Station in the district of Paschim Medinipur of the district of West Bengal in Republic of India. However, its heritage status is undisputed as the temples are more than hundred years old and have recently been renovated by the family of Kar Sarkars who have been maintaining these temples since inception.
